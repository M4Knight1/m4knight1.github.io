PERSONAL PROJECT

Displayed here is my personal project: Headphone Speakers and Design. This page displays the design concepts as well as some insight into how my project works (as well as other projects).

A speaker works by converting electrical signals into physical vibrations that create sound waves, using a powerful magnet, a voice coil, and a flexible cone (diaphragm). The audio signal causes the voice coil to rapidly move back and forth within the magnet's field, making the attached cone vibrate and push air, producing the sound we hear.
